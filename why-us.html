Company Overview

At Ultra Medical Cleaning & Environmental Services, we stand out for our unwavering commitment to excellence in every aspect of our operations. Our rigorous 5-year background checks and extensive 5-day training program ensure that our team is not only skilled but also dedicated to delivering the highest quality service. We go above and beyond regulatory requirements, implementing strict compliance measures such as random drug testing and maintaining liability insurance exceeding $2 million.

With a total quality approach at our core, we continuously strive for improvement, focusing on asking the right questions, gathering accurate information, and refining our methods. Our comprehensive training regimen, encompassing audio-visual modules, hands-on instructions, and on-the-job training, equips our employees with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in their roles and work seamlessly as part of an efficient team.

This emphasis on standardized training and continuous improvement allows us to provide consistent, top-notch service to our clients, ensuring their satisfaction and peace of mind.
